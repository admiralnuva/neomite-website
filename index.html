Policies

Clear terms for business software.

These short policy sections are written for the review version of the site. Final legal language should be reviewed before production launch.

Privacy Policy

Neomite collects information needed to provide our software products, support users, manage accounts, and process subscriptions.

  • Account details such as name, email, company, and login information.
  • Uploaded documents and business data used to provide product analysis.
  • Billing details handled by Stripe or another payment provider.
  • Usage data used to improve reliability, support, and product quality.

Terms of Service

Neomite provides software tools for business workflow support. Customers are responsible for reviewing outputs and making final business decisions.

  • Use the products only for lawful business purposes.
  • Do not upload content you are not authorized to use.
  • AI-assisted outputs may be incomplete or incorrect and should be reviewed.
  • Neomite does not guarantee contract awards, financial outcomes, or compliance results.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Customers may cancel a subscription at any time. Access continues through the current billing period unless otherwise stated.

  • Monthly subscription payments are generally non-refundable after access is provided.
  • Refunds may be considered for duplicate charges, billing errors, or required legal exceptions.
  • Free trials and free tiers may have usage limits.
  • Subscription changes are handled through the product billing flow or support.

Security & Data Handling

Neomite products are designed for business users who upload sensitive operational documents and financial information.

  • Customer data is used to provide the requested product functionality.
  • Access is limited to authorized users and service providers.
  • Documents are not sold to advertisers or unrelated third parties.
  • Customers may request account or data deletion through support.

This page is not legal advice. Before accepting payments or launching publicly, Neomite Labs LLC should finalize policies with appropriate legal review.
